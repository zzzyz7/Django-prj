About

We will implement a website for adopting pets, pet lost claim, lost pet find, find a new owner for your pet, cloud adoption pets and ask for help on pet-related knowledge. Since many people like to keep pets, especially those who are lonely. Our goal is to give people who need pets a better platform to adopt pets. At the same time, it also gives people who can no longer keep pets a good platform to communicate with the next owner of their pet.
